Refund Policy

This Policy consists of the procedure to be followed and terms and conditions applied and considered for Refund and Cancellation in regards to Services provided by ATHWISE Services

Definitions

  • Service Provider: The person who is giving specified services in exchange for a payment.
  • Service Receiver: Service receiver is a person who receives or avails the services provided by the service provider.

Eligibility Requirements For Refund

The Service receiver will be eligible to claim refund only after a thorough assessment of the service in question was made by the Service Provider or the same is stated in the duly executed documents such as Agreement, Undertaking, or any such other documents signed by both Service Provider and Service Receiver depending on the circumstances.

Types Of Refunds

Refund will include such amount as mentioned in the duly executed documents or any such amount mutually agreed between the Service Provider and Service Receiver.

Refund Timeframe

Refund will include such amount as mentioned in the duly executed documents or any such amount mutually agreed between the Service Provider and Service Receiver.

Refund Process

An email will be dispatched from ATHWISE Services, outlining the terms and conditions governing the refund process. This communication will include comprehensive details regarding the criteria for refund eligibility, along with the specific reason for the refund and the corresponding refund amount.

Upon receipt of this email, the recipient of the service is required to review and acknowledge the terms stated therein. Additionally, the service recipient must furnish their bank account particulars, including the account number and IFSC code of the relevant branch, to facilitate the transfer of the refund amount by ATHWISE Services.

Once the necessary details are received, ATHWISE Services will initiate the refund process without delay. The refund amount will be disbursed to the service recipient within the stipulated timeframe, as specified in the email communication (i.e., within 45 working days from the date of receiving all requisite documents, details, and information).

Grievance Resolution

In case of any query or clarity required by the Service Receiver, the Company or authorized representative of the Company can mail on support@athwise.in through their mail id provided to us for service-related communications stating the details such as Name of the Company, Service availed, Query, Name, and contact of the person connected with us on the project.

Note

Payments for services are only accepted in the name of ATHWISE Services Private Limited, the type of account is solely “Current Account” via NEFT/IMPS, RTGS, and digital payment wallets (Cash Free, Razor pay, Aggrepay, Google Pay, Phone Pay, Paytm, Credit Card, Debit Card), and we do not accept payments on personal accounts or under any other name.
